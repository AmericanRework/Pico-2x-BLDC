Additional documentation:


To connect external 3.3v: use the 3.3v in / vsys pin (upper right corner of the pico if usb is top) It's labeled on the board.


Pinout for Husqvarna Automower 450x cut motor to match the drive motor 8 pin molex (drive motor comes with a 10 pin molex but only 8 pins used): 

-Plug latch on top. View looking at circuit board/back of plug:
-First row: black-gnd, blue-U, white-V, green-W
-Second row: red-3.3v, yellow-hallU, grey-hallV, purple-hallW

Gives same electrical angle as the drive motors. Our pinout is written directly on the board to make wiring up motors easy.
